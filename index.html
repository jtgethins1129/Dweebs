Your Neighborhood Living Room

Dweebs prides itself on being a welcoming neighborhood cafe. Our mission is to provide the best in coffee in breakfast that Bushwick has to offer.

At Dweebs we work with a variety of roasters to offer an always changing selection of the most flavorful coffee the city has to offer. Paired with our wide selection of pastries, as well as breakfast sandwiches hot off the grill. Dweebs is your one stop shop for everything you need to start your morning.
