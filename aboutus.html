Dweebs

Dweebs was founded in 2012 by two Brooklyn baristas who decided to go their own way. Finding a space of their own in the heart of Bushwick the duo set out to create a home for coffee lovers and creatives in the neighborhood. Providing atmosphere and great eats, Dweebs is the perfect place for you and your friends to gather.
